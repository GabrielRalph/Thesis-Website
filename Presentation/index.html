Conclusions

  • Developed a fast and easy to use simulation environment for active reconstruction tasks.
  • Implemented collision detection, LIDAR simulation, and coverage calculation modules.
  • Integrated with a reinforcement learning framework to train an active reconstruction agent.
  • Obtained initial training results demonstrating the viability of the approach.

Future Improvements

  • Further optimisations can be made to improve ray casting and collision detection.
  • More work is need to shape the reward landscape of the RL policy.
  • Explore different reinforcement learning algorithms and architectures.
  • Conduct more evaluations in real-world scenarios.
